The second edition keeps the narrative spine and sharpens the public promise:
context collapse is the diagnosis; formation after context collapse is the work.

Key changes:

- Panta Rhei is now published and routed as an optional formal bridge.
- AI is treated as a formation challenge, not only as productivity technology.
- The appendix materials have become companion papers and field kits.
- The source apparatus has moved toward a versioned companion compendium.
- The five-framework operating system now anchors reader navigation: Value Creation Map, Four Rings of Context, Decision Rights and Healthy Hierarchy, Conflict Infrastructure, and Formation Ladder.
