AI compresses output loops. Without protected formation, organizations may get more artifacts and fewer people who understand how to judge them.

This is the second edition's sharper 2026 argument: AI is not only a productivity story. It is a formation story.

## What AI Can Accelerate

- Drafting, summarization, translation, pattern search, and routine synthesis.
- Prototype loops where source material and evaluation criteria are clear.
- Workflows where an accountable human still owns judgment.

## What AI Can Hollow Out

- Entry-level exposure to source reality.
- Apprenticeship paths where people learn by doing partial work.
- Judgment formation when outputs arrive faster than review.
- Conflict and appeal paths when algorithmic output shapes human decisions.

## The Minimum Formation Ladder

Before automating a junior task, name what the task used to teach. The release-candidate manuscript proposes a simple starting artifact: source reality, human hypothesis, evidence comparison, tradeoff explanation, and accountable recommendation.
