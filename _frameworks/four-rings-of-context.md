Not every problem is a communication problem. Many are context problems.

The Four Rings give teams a way to name which context is missing before escalating, meeting, or blaming. Each ring updates at a different speed, and collapse begins when all layers are forced to move at the same frequency.

## The Rings

- **Purpose:** why the work deserves attention.
- **Principles:** what must remain true while the work changes.
- **Playfield:** which constraints, roles, facts, and boundaries govern the work now.
- **State:** what is happening, who owns it, and what needs action next.

## First Move

Before the next stalled decision, ask which ring is missing, which one is being updated too fast, and which one no one owns.
