Conflict does not disappear when it has no path. It becomes silence, politics, exit, or moral theater.

Conflict Infrastructure makes disagreement legitimate enough to carry truth and bounded enough not to burn the house down.

## First Move

Define the minimum viable conflict path:

1. Signal.
2. Classify.
3. Route.
4. Protect.
5. Decide or repair.
6. Record.
7. Review.
