Hierarchy is not domination by default. It becomes domination when authority loses boundaries, voice, review, and competence.

Healthy hierarchy gives work back its decision paths. It makes ownership visible, review legitimate, and escalation less political.

## First Move

List the recurring decisions your team reopens most often. For each decision type, name the owner, required evidence, escalation path, and review rhythm.
