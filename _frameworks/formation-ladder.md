AI can accelerate output. It cannot replace the formation path through which people learn what good output is.

The Formation Ladder protects exposure, practice, feedback, accountability, and judgment before automation compresses the old first tasks into invisible artifacts.

## First Move

Choose one AI-exposed junior role. Write the first five rungs: observe source reality, draft a hypothesis before AI, compare AI output against sources, explain a tradeoff to a human, and own a recommendation.
